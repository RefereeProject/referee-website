# Referee

Referee is founder-led research-integrity infrastructure for scholarly publishing. It produces transparent, machine-readable reliability signals for structured triage and targeted expert review.

## The problem
Submission volume is increasing while intake review capacity does not scale at the same pace. Integrity checks are fragmented, and review outputs are often difficult to audit.

## The solution
Referee structures screening signals into an explicit evaluation record that maps reliability signals to flaw categories and unresolved checks.

## How it works
1. Ingest paper evidence
2. Map checks to CRWE categories
3. Record checked vs unresolved items
4. Produce reliability signals for triage

## Who it is for
- Conference organizers
- Publishers and journals
- Research organizations
