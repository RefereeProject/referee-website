# Academic collegiality is a bug, not a feature (and it's largely not true)

Date: 2025-05-07

Academia often claims collegiality, but real peer-review incentives can suppress open criticism. This article argues that stronger research evaluation requires more direct, less gatekept critique and broader participation beyond traditional credential boundaries.
